Frequently asked questions

How do I add a reminder?

Tap the orange + button at the bottom-right of the home screen. Type the occasion, pick a date, choose a category and color, and tap "Save this day".

How do I edit or delete a reminder?

Long-press any reminder card on the home screen to bring up the paper menu, then choose Edit or Delete.

Will notifications still work if I close the app?

Yes. Dayly schedules notifications locally on your device, so they fire even when the app isn't open. Make sure notifications are enabled in iOS Settings → Notifications → Dayly, and that Focus modes aren't filtering them out.

How do I remove ads?

Open Settings (the gear icon on the home screen) and tap "Go ad-free". The one-time purchase removes all banner, native, and interstitial ads across the app.

I bought "Remove Ads" on another device. How do I restore it?

Open Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you used when you originally purchased.

How do I add the widget to my home screen?

Long-press an empty spot on your iPhone or iPad home screen, tap the + in the top-left, search for "Dayly", choose a size (small, medium, or large), and tap "Add Widget". Tap and hold the widget to choose which reminder it shows.

My data — is it private?

Yes. Your reminders are stored on your device. We don't have an account system and don't upload your content to a server. See our Privacy Policy for details.
