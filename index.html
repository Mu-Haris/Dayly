What is Dayly?

Dayly turns the dates that matter into a scrapbook you'll actually want to open. Pin birthdays, anniversaries, holidays, and one-off milestones to a paper-themed home screen and watch the days flip by. Notifications keep you a step ahead, and a widget brings your next big day right to your home screen.
