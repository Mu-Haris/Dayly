Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Kinex Apps ("we", "us", "our") built the Dayly app ("the App") as a Freemium app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using Dayly you agree to the practices described below.

Information stored on your device

Dayly is designed to keep your data on your device. All reminders you create — titles, dates, categories, notes, colors, and notification preferences — are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's standard on-device storage. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to this content.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or contacts.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not access your photos, calendar, or location.

Information collected by third-party services

Dayly uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Google AdMob (advertising)

The free version of Dayly shows advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier (IDFA) and limited technical information about your device (model, OS version, language, approximate location based on IP) to serve and measure ads. Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt is shown the first time you launch the app — you may decline tracking at any time, and the App will continue to function. Personalized ads are only requested if you allow tracking.

For details and to manage your ad preferences, see Google's policy at policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Google User Messaging Platform (consent)

We use the Google UMP SDK to display a consent form to users in regions where it is required (such as the EEA and UK) and to record your consent preferences.

Apple StoreKit (in-app purchases)

If you make an in-app purchase (for example, to remove ads), the transaction is processed by Apple. We receive a confirmation that a purchase took place but we do not receive your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to this transaction.

Notifications

Dayly may ask permission to send local notifications so it can remind you of your upcoming days. These notifications are scheduled and delivered entirely on your device. You can disable them at any time in iOS Settings.

Widget & App Group

To power the home-screen widget, Dayly shares your reminders with its own widget extension through an Apple App Group on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Children's privacy

Dayly is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your rights

Because we don't collect personal data on our servers, there is no account to delete or data to export from us. To remove all reminder data, simply delete the App from your device. If you have allowed ad tracking and wish to revoke consent, you can do so in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or by resetting consent inside Dayly's Settings screen.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date.
